Berachain Bridge (Bera Bridge) – The Fastest Way to Move Assets Across Blockchains

The Berachain Bridge is the official, secure, and most reliable way to transfer digital assets across blockchains. Whether you're moving tokens into Berachain or sending them back out, our bridge ensures seamless, low-cost, and lightning-fast transactions.

Unlike generic cross-chain tools, the Bera Bridge is purpose-built for Berachain, giving you enterprise-grade security, near-zero gas fees, and unmatched performance.

What is a Bridge Token?

A bridge token is a digital asset that represents value transferred from one blockchain to another. Berabridge locks tokens on the source chain and mints equivalent tokens on the destination chain, enabling seamless value exchange and cross-chain compatibility.
